# Deploy a Database

To deploy a WeaveDB database, follow these steps:

<Steps>
### Step 1
 
Go to [console.weavedb.dev](https://console.weavedb.dev/).
 
### Step 2
 
Click on the `Deploy WeaveDB` button.

![](https://i.imgur.com/4kzNNZr.png)

### Step 3

Connect your wallet by clicking on `Connect Owner Wallet`. This wallet will serve as the admin account for configuring your database.

![](https://i.imgur.com/dSZfEQ1.png)

### Step 4

Set `Secure` to `False` for the purposes of this tutorial. 

<Callout type="warning" emoji="⚠️">
In a production environment, you should set `Secure` to `True`.
</Callout>

### Step 5

Click on `Deploy DB Instance`. You can view the transaction for the deployment by clicking on the `contractTxId` link.

![](https://i.imgur.com/vL4d75W.png)

### Step 6

Finally, click on the `Sign Into DB` button to sign in with your owner wallet.

![](https://i.imgur.com/UDaASKa.png)

</Steps>
